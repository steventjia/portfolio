Lead UI/UX Start-Up Journey at RiddleStory, Netherlands

About The Journey

As Lead UI/UX Designer for RiddleStory, the number one onboarding platform in the Netherlands, I handle all design work—products, dashboards, websites, games, puzzles, branding, and the design system. I also led the rebranding to RiddleStory 2.0, giving the platform a fresh and modern identity. Working with a team of two interns, I’ve gained valuable experience collaborating with people from different countries & cultures.

Main Responsibilities

  1. Design the products, dashboards, websites, mobile apps, games, puzzles, etc.
  2. Build and maintain the design system.
  3. Lead the rebranding to RiddleStory 2.0.
  4. Guide and mentor two design interns.

Design Flow

My design process begins with brainstorming sessions with the Product Manager or CTO to define the project flow and create a detailed PRD. For UX Research, I conduct Quantitative Research by gathering data from user reviews and feedback, and Qualitative Research such as competitor analysis and SWOT assessments to uncover opportunities and pain points. I then move on to wireframing and crafting high-fidelity designs, followed by iterations based on feedback. Once the design is refined, I create prototypes to bring the concept to life and ensure functionality. Finally, I present the completed work to the team or CEO for approval and alignment.

The Problems before I joined the company

RiddleStory 1.0 had a limited focus, offering only two products: the Story and a rather outdated dashboard. Many features were incomplete, and there was a clear need to add more functionality to improve the user experience. Additionally, the branding lacked consistency, with mismatched colors, fonts, and spacing, which made the platform feel disconnected and less polished. These issues highlighted the need for a major overhaul to provide a more cohesive and feature-rich user experience.

How Can I Contribute as a Lead UI/UX Designer?

As a Lead UI/UX Designer, my role is to contribute by generating fresh ideas for new features and products, including mobile apps, websites, and revamping the old dashboard. I also focus on designing engaging environments, such as the game room, ensuring that every aspect of the platform is visually appealing and user-friendly. Additionally, I work to maintain a consistent design system that aligns with the rebranding efforts for RiddleStory 2.0, ensuring a cohesive experience across all products and platforms. My goal is to elevate the user experience while supporting the company's vision and growth.
