Mobile App Design for One Base Health App, Australia

About The Project

This freelance project, which I secured from a client in Australia, revolves around Onebase Health—an exciting health app that's all about helping people lead healthier and happier lives. With Onebase Health, users get an all-in-one platform to manage their well-being. It lets you easily keep track of things like your health metrics and fitness goals, and it even connects you with caring therapists for those days when you need a little extra support. Plus, the app offers loads of useful health tips and information. My role in this project is to make the app more user-friendly and efficient, and I'm truly excited to be part of the team working to empower individuals in their journey to better health and well-being through Onebase Health.

Objectives

  1. Enhance Accessibility to Therapy Sessions: The primary objective of building the Onebase Health mobile app is to provide individuals with easy and immediate access to therapy sessions. This includes improving the availability of certified therapists, counselors, and mental health professionals for remote consultations, thereby reducing barriers to mental health care.
  2. Seamless User Experience: To create a user-friendly and intuitive interface that ensures a seamless experience for users seeking therapy sessions or utilizing other health management features. This involves optimizing the app's navigation, scheduling, and secure communication channels between users and therapists / customer service.
  3. Data Security and Privacy: Prioritize the security and privacy of user data and interactions within the app. This includes robust encryption measures, strict data access controls, and compliance with relevant data protection regulations, such as GDPR or HIPAA, to ensure that users can trust Onebase Health with their sensitive health information and therapy sessions.
  4. Continuous Improvement and Feedback Integration: Establish mechanisms for collecting user feedback and monitoring app performance closely. Regularly analyze user feedback to identify areas for improvement, whether related to therapy session quality, app functionality, or content relevance. This continuous improvement approach ensures that Onebase Health evolves to meet the changing needs of its users.

Methodology

For The UX Research Methods, i use both Quantitative Research to gather informations from studies, reviews from users who used other competitors’ Health App / Theraphy Sessions App and also Qualitative Research to do competitor analysis to find the main problems and to compare strength and weakness from each other’s Health / Theraphy Sessions App (Betterhelp, Talkspace, Apple Health).

UX Research Process

Joining this project midway, my approach to understanding user experiences has been a blend of quantitative and qualitative research methods with a human touch. On the quantitative side, I've been digging into studies and user reviews from people who have engaged with various Health App and Therapy Sessions Apps offered by our competitors. These insights give us a broad and data-backed view of what users appreciate and what they find challenging.

Additionally, I've taken a deep dive into qualitative research by meticulously analyzing our competitors' apps. This has involved identifying the core issues users face and meticulously comparing the strengths and weaknesses of each Health App. This qualitative approach goes beyond numbers, allowing us to uncover the nuanced aspects that can profoundly impact user satisfaction. By harmonizing these two research approaches, we're crafting a more empathetic and user-centric experience for our Health App.

What are the key considerations when designing a mobile app for therapy sessions and health management?

When creating a mobile app for therapy sessions and health management, several critical considerations come into play. First and foremost, you must prioritize data security and privacy to protect users' sensitive health and therapy-related information. Additionally, the app's user interface should be intuitive and user-friendly, ensuring that individuals of varying tech-savviness can easily navigate it. Providing options for customization and personalization is also essential, as users often have unique health needs and preferences. Finally, seamless integration of therapy sessions, educational resources, and health tracking features is crucial for offering a holistic user experience.

How can a mobile app for therapy sessions and health app improve overall well-being?

A mobile app designed for therapy sessions and health management can significantly enhance overall well-being in several ways. It provides convenient access to certified therapists and counselors, breaking down geographical barriers and making mental health support more accessible. Users can track their physical health metrics, set and achieve health goals, and access valuable educational content, empowering them to make informed decisions about their well-being. The integration of mental health and physical health features fosters a holistic approach to wellness, addressing both mental and physical aspects. Ultimately, such an app can empower individuals to take control of their health, leading to improved mental and physical well-being.

The Process

1. Health App / Therapy Sessions App References

For Health App/Therapy Session App references, I studied and compared competitors’ apps. Specifically, I analyzed Apple Health, Betterhealth, and Talkspace. I assessed their contents, protocols, scheduling and also the user interface.

2. Challenges & Possible Solutions

One of the key challenges for the OneBase health app is maintaining consistent adherence to personalized health protocols. Users may face difficulties in staying motivated and following their scheduled sessions regularly, leading to incomplete or irregular protocol execution. Possible Solutions.

To address this challenge, the OneBase app can employ gamification techniques, rewarding users for completing sessions and making protocol adherence engaging. Implementing push notifications and reminders can help users stay on track, with personalized messages based on their progress. Additionally, a progress tracking feature can visually display their achievements, while the app's social component enables users to connect with peers for support and motivation. Lastly, providing educational content within the app about the benefits of protocol adherence can further encourage users to stay committed to their health goals.
