Tablet Design for Gudang Garam Catalogue App, Indonesia

About The Project

This project involves the development of the Gudang Garam Merah Tablet App, which I undertook while employed at FAB Indonesia, a digital agency, in a full-time capacity. My role in this project centered around designing the tablet app for an E-Catalogue, enabling users to effortlessly access information about products associated with the Gudang Garam Merah Event. This information encompasses details about booths, units, as well as merchandise offerings.

Objectives

  1. User-Friendly Interface: Create an intuitive and user-friendly interface to ensure that users can easily navigate the app, search for products, and access information about Gudang Garam Merah Event offerings without any confusion or frustration.
  2. Comprehensive Product Showcase: Develop a feature-rich E-Catalogue that showcases a wide range of products related to the Gudang Garam Merah Event, including booths, units, and merchandise. Ensure that product descriptions, images, and specifications are easily accessible and well-organized within the app.
  3. Offline Accessibility: Implement functionality that allows users to access the E-Catalogue and browse products even when they are offline. This feature ensures uninterrupted access to critical information, especially in environments with limited connectivity.
  4. Tutorial and Assembly Guidance: Offer step-by-step tutorials and assembly instructions within the app to assist users in understanding and assembling the products associated with the Gudang Garam Merah Event.

Methodology

For The UX Research Methods, i use both Quantitative Research to gather informations from studies, reviews from users who used other competitors’ E-Catalogue App and also Qualitative Research to do competitor analysis to find the main problems and to compare strength and weakness from each other’s E-Catalogue App (Catalog Machine, Tokopedia, Zoho Catalog).

UX Research Process

I collect information through quantitative research by studying and reviewing user feedback from competitors' E-Catalogue Apps. This process aids in my understanding of user preferences and market challenges. As a result of my research, I can identify key issues and conduct a thorough comparison of the strengths and weaknesses of each app. This approach provides valuable insights that enable me to enhance the user experience and distinguish our app in the market.

What are some key things to keep in mind when designing the user interface for a product catalog table app?

When you're designing the user interface for your product catalog table app, think about simplicity and user-friendliness. Make sure the layout is easy to understand, so users can quickly find the products they're looking for. Use clear navigation and filters to help users refine their searches easily. And don't forget about responsive design – it ensures a smooth experience on different devices and screen sizes.

How do you make sure the product information in your catalog app is accurate and consistent, especially when product details change frequently?

Keeping your product information accurate and consistent in a dynamic catalog app is all about good data management. First, set up a central database where you regularly update and sync product data. Use version control and audit trails to track changes and maintain data integrity. To prevent errors and keep things consistent, establish clear roles and workflows for data entry and validation. Also, schedule regular data audits to remove duplicates and outdated info – it'll help keep your catalog in top shape.

The Process

1. E-Catalogue References

For E-Catalogue references, I studied and compared the apps/websites of E-Catalogue/Product Catalogue. Specifically, I analyzed Tokopedia, Catalog Machine, and Zoho Catalog. I assessed their design, user-friendliness, load times, placement of contents, visuals, call-to-action buttons, and any standout features.

2. Challenges & Possible Solutions

Building a product catalogue app is a rewarding endeavor, yet it comes with its share of challenges that i've addressed with practical solutions. User-friendliness is paramount, and i have put a lot of thought into ensuring an intuitive interface. We've simplified navigation, integrated powerful search and filter features, and optimized responsiveness across different devices to enhance the user experience. In summary, this E-Catalogue app is designed to overcome challenges by focusing on an improved user experience, real-time data management, and robust security measures to provide users with a reliable and secure platform for exploring and discovering products.
